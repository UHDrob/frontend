Self-portrait in a Straw Hat

By Louise Elizabeth Lebrun

The painting appears, after cleaning, to be an authograph replica of apicture, the original of which was painted in Brusels in 1782 in free imitation of Ruben's Chapeau de Pasille, which LeBrun had seen in Antwerp. It was exhibited in Paris in 1782 at the Salon de la Corresponcence. LeBrun's original is recorded in a private colleciton in France
